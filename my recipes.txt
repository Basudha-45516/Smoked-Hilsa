Ingredients required:4pieces of hilsa fish,mustard powder(50g), greenchili paste(1/2 spoon),mustard oil(4 teaspoon),greenchili(4/5),Haldi powder(1 spoon),salt(1/2spoon),curd(2 teaspoon).

Preparations:We will take 4 pieces of hilsa fish merinated with the above ingredients except green chili.After that we have to leave it for five minutes. After that we have to bake it for 5 minutes(180.c)And serve it with 4 or 5 green chilis.And your Smoked hilsa fish is ready.
